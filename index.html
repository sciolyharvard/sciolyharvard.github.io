About Us

The Harvard College Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament will be held on Sunday, January 28, 2018 at Harvard University in Cambridge, MA. Our Division C event will feature 30 teams competing in all 23 events. Competition events will run from approximately 7:00am to 3:00pm with all closing ceremony-related functions finishing by 6:00pm assuming no delays.

Eligibility & Registration

  • Teams must be registered with their state Science Olympiad organization prior to competing in the Harvard Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament, and must conform to the Science Olympiad Division C membership rules found here.
  • Schools may register two teams; however, the second team will be placed on a waitlist.
  • All competing students must conform to the eligibility rules found here.
  • Registration will be capped at 30 teams, and registration will be closed once 30 teams have registered.

Competition Day

  • All students must sign (1) a liability waiver, (2) a health form, (3) a media release form, prior to competing in the Harvard Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament. Forms will be provided to coaches after registration is confirmed.
  • Unless otherwise stated, the Harvard Invitational Tournament will adhere to all rules stated in the national rule book and national event clarifications posted here. In cases which the printed national rule book specifies different rules or content for different competition levels, the Harvard Invitational Tournament will follow the the rules specified for the regional tournament level
  • Every team will be given access to a homeroom in Sever Hall (in Harvard Yard)
  • Each team must be accompanied to the Harvard Invitational tournament by one coach, and one chaperone (two unique adults)
  • Teams must abide by the official Code of Ethics and General Rules specified here

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register?

Teams must be registered with their state Science Olympiad organization prior to competing in the Harvard Science Olympiad Invitational Tournament, and must conform to the Science Olympiad Division C membership rules found here.

Why is the registration fee $150?

Our event is hosted in Harvard Yard which is the center of the oldest part of Harvard University’s campus. We understand that our registration fee is on the high end for invitationals; this is to ensure venue costs don’t inhibit us from properly executing on the other aspects of the invitational.

How will parking on campus work?

As our event takes place on a Sunday, street parking in the City of Cambridge will be free of charge.

When can I register?

Registration will open in September. For now, sign up for updates using the button up above and we will let you know when a registration date is announced.

How big can my team be?

We allow teams of up to 15 high school students, no more than 7 of whom can be seniors. Every team must have at least one dedicated coach, and one additional chaperone.

When will you release room assignments, event schedule, team list, etc.?

Once all teams are registered, we will be able to provide room assignments and other information. At latest, this will be three weeks before the event. We will send an email to team coaches when this information is finalized.
